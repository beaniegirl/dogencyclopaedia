The Dog Encyclopedia

If you are looking for a canine companion but are not able to decide which breed to choose, you are in the right place!! The information you find here will help you understand that adding a dog to your family is a big responsibility and this website will guide you through the process of choosing a breed that suits your liking and lifestyle.
All breeds are different and don't have the same nutritional and exercise needs and require different levels of maintenance and grooming.

The health issues that dogs are most likely to develop as they age, vary from breed to breed. You can find everything you need to know about the different breeds here.
Hope the dog lover in you enjoys this online experience.
